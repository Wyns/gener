How Gener works

Most "broadband" services are not managed. Just fibre optic-and-copper cabling from the exchange to your street, then copper phone wires into your building's common router. And that really spoils the fun when you are not assured of a constant speed to your gadget. As a technology, it's time to upgrade.

Gener specialises in bringing broadband direct to multi-dwelling buildings such as apartments and offices. If your building is within our catchment area, and enough residents show support by registering online, we can connect you to our future-proof network.

How we install:

  • Enough people register interest online
  • We connect cabling directly to your building
  • Gener router is installed discretely to each home or unit
  • Each user connects via an individual/home/unit Gener account

And that's it. Our technicians keep your service running smoothly, and our customer service team is always here to help 24/7

The Gener difference

Gener Wi-fi broadband is true broadband. It pipes Kenya's fastest residential speeds all the way to your building, using fibre optic or copper cabling direct from the exchange.

Not only does Gener ensure you get the speed you pay for, but it stays reliably fast - even during peak times.

Better still, you can upload as fast as you download on the speed you pay for.

What's the problems with other broadband services are speed, reliability and value for money. For example, the average standard broadband providers promise an "up to " speed, which you pay for but can't get.

What's worse, the broadband is not so great for downloading HD movies, uploading huge files, or playing the latest games.
